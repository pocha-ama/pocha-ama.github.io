## Research Interests
My research focuses on **Collective Intelligence** and the **Design of Epistemic Democracy**. I use agent-based modeling and behavioral experiments to study:
<i class="fas fa-angle-right"></i> &nbsp; When collective intelligence emerges and when it breaks down.
<i class="fas fa-angle-right"></i> &nbsp; The mechanisms of consensus-building and group decision-making.
<i class="fas fa-angle-right"></i> &nbsp; How institutions can be designed to support collective intelligence.
